Manifesto

  • Technology
    Revolutionize our local government through creation of technological and innovation hubs in each LCDA and support local startups through access to seed funding
  • Security
    Intensify security through neighbourhood watch organisations, CCTV monitoring and vigilante groups
  • Agriculture
    Advance and support the agricultural sector through farmer's markets, grants of farm implements and fertilizers to farmers
  • Women & Youth
    Women and youth empowerment through grants, skill acquisition and co-operative societies
  • Sports
    Promote the engagement in sports by establishing a domestic sport council and training center
  • Economy
    Facilitate the growth of our economic base through the establishment of industries
  • Education
    Enhance the educational sector through a total revamp of our educational curriculum and mandatory establishment of Information and Communication Technology (ICT) centers
  • Flooding
    Initiate proper drainage systems to address and subsequently stop the issue of flooding
  • Health
    Strengthen primary healthcare through supplying state of the art tools and facilities and upskilling the medical workers with new and innovative skills
