Welcome to the party of champions

We, at NNPP, recognize the need to promote and preserve he unity, peace, progress and sovereignty of the Federal Republic of Nigeria, as well as enkindle in Nigerians a deep sense of patriotism with a view to engendering true federalism;


Observing the need to mobilize all credible persons, professionals, youths, women and all Nigerians, with the fear of God, for the purpose of taking over the control of government of the federal Republic of Nigeria, through democratic elections.

About NNDP

Believing that it is the responsibility of Nigerians to ensure the repositioning of our great country under the fear of God, with total commitment; Building on the ideology of democratic liberalism, we shall positively impact on mechanized agriculture and surplus food production; rural development; manufacturing industries; energy; mining and Petrochemical industry: iron and steel development; transportation with emphasis on rail-realization, airways and waterways; maintenance engineering, science and technology: telecommunications and information technology: labour employment and wages with emphasis on full employment: mass qualitative education;

Our Motto: The motto of the party shall be PEACE, EQUITY AND PROGRESS

Our Candidates

Bilkisu Dauda-Damisa

Presidential Candidate

She is a seasoned public servant with many years of experience in service. She is an advocate of good governance and right of girl-child. She is currently an SA M&E to the Hon. Minister of Humanitarian Affairs, Disaster Management and Social Development. She is a wife, mother and a close knitted family oriented person.

Gyang Dafwa Hans

Vice Presidental Candidate

He hails from Plateau State, Nigeria. He is a Pharmacist with interest in Business Management and Cooperate/Civil Leadership.He has led youth organizations and businesses to great heights over the years and He is presently the Managing Director of Noro Pharmacy and Health Shop Ltd. His Passion has now shifted to public service, hence dedicating my time and energy into directing the course of Nigeria’s leadership challenge to a more inclusive and progressive one for the sake of liberating the Nigerian people from the shackles of bad governance.

Eruoghene Iroro

Local Government Candidate

She is a software engineer with a firm belief in the importance of grassroot politics. She is an avid advocate of the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals(SDGs), with a plethora of voluntary and appointed leadership positions. Her experience in leadership as well as her tech expertise will provide a unique and fresh perspective for the growth of our local governments.
